Montréal, Québec




Montréal is the only fully bilingual city in the French Canadian province of Québec. It's known for it's diversity, art and the never-ending construction. Mount Royal - the 230m mountain within the city from which the city gets its name - allows for a quick escape into nature with stunning views of Montreal from the top. In the winter, Beaver Lake on the summit is turned into a skating rink which you can get to by cross-country skiing up along the mountain, if you're feeling adventurous!
